Name Chi-Squared
Definition Used to understand the relationship between two categorical variables using frequency (count). It compares observed to expected.
Hypothese \( H_0 \): The two variables are independent. Variation is due to chance
\( H_a \): The two variables are not independent. Variation is not due to chance
Formula \( \chi^2 = \Sigma \dfrac{(observed - expected)^2}{expected} \)
Input Values Observed Count, Expected Count
Output Values
