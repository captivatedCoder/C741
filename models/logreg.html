Name Logistic Regression
Definition A method used to examine the association of independent variables (categorical or continuous) with one dichotomous (y/n, t/f, 0/1) dependent variable.
Hypothese \( h_0: B_1 = 0 \)
\( h_a: B_1 \ne 0 \)
Formulas LogReg Function \( E(X) = \pi(X) \) = \( \dfrac{e^{B_0+B_1X}}{{1+e^{B_0+B_1X}}} \)

LogReg Model \( E(X) = \pi(X) + \epsilon \) = \( \dfrac{e^{B_0+B_1X}}{{1+{e^B_0+B_1X}}} + \epsilon \)

logit(p) \( \ln \left( \dfrac{p}{(1-p)} \right) \)
