R - correlation coefficient says if there is a linear relationship
\( \hat y_i = b_0 + {b_1}{x_1} \)
Slope - \( b_1 = \dfrac{\Sigma(x_i - \bar x)(y_i - \bar y)}{\Sigma(x_i - \bar x)} \)
Y-Int - \( b_0 = \bar y - b_1\bar x \) Centroid - \( \mu_x, \mu_y \) Line MUST pass through this point. Least Squares Method
\( min \; \Sigma \; (y_i - \hat y_i)^2 \)
